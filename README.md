# GitHub Foundations Certification Practice Quiz

A free, self-contained practice quiz to help you prepare for the **GitHub Foundations** certification exam.

👉 **Take the quiz:** https://ggingo.github.io/github-foundations-cert-quiz/

## How it works

- Each question is presented one at a time with single-answer (radio) or multi-answer (checkbox) options.
- Pick your answer(s) and press **Submit**.
  - **Correct** → your choice turns green and you're told you got it right.
  - **Incorrect / partial** → wrong picks turn red, the correct answer is shown in green.
- Missed questions are **randomly re-inserted** into the queue so you keep practicing them until you get them right.
- When the queue is empty you get a **summary**: total questions answered and your percentage correct.

## Features

- ✅ Runs entirely in your browser — no install, no account, works offline once loaded.
- 💾 **Auto-save & resume** — your progress is saved to your browser automatically. Close the tab and come back later to pick up where you left off.
- ↻ **Restart** any time from the button in the top-right.
- 🔒 Progress is stored locally on your own device (it is never uploaded), and each person's progress is independent.

## Using it offline

Prefer to run it without internet? Download `index.html` from this repo and double-click it — it opens in any browser and behaves identically.
